Agriculture For
Future Development

RICH FARMS AGRICULTURAL COOPERATIVE SOCIETY NIGERIA, expanded to Rich-Farms Agricultural Investment Development Company Limited, with RC No. 7862683 [FCTA/ARDS/COOP/2019/3523, Federal Republic of Nigeria] is registered Agricultural Company and Cooperative Society in Nigeria dedicated to improving the economic well-being and quality of life of Nigerian Youths, Women in Rural Areas, Our society operates throughout Nigeria, with a mission to empower Farmers, Agricultural Producers, and Rural Communities through collective action, shared resources and expertise with over 78,000 members including corporate organizations, serving/retired civil servants, military and Paramilitary, National Youth Service Corps.

Our cooperative amongst other things, specializes in Agriculture management consultancy and development services both Nationally and Internationally, supporting Government Agencies, Organizations and Corporate bodies by offering specialized services in National and International program exchange of ideas and capacity building to Government, Organizations and Entities going through Agricultural, Economic and Structural change.

Under this creation, our Vision and Aims is to galvanize and encourage the civil servants in Ministries, Department and Agencies, [MDAs] by mobilizing them into society of Farming Communities for small, medium and larger scale farming with the introduction of Modern and Mechanized Farming Technics using the platform.

A move so highly embraced by the Nigerian Military, Paramilitary, The National Youth Service Corp Members, Senior Staff Association of Nigeria Universities/Polytechnics, Raw Material Research and Development Institute, Federal Ministry of Agriculture and Food Security, Federal Capital Territory Administration, Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Nigeria Association of Chambers of Commerce, Industry, Mines and Agriculture, Nigeria Labour Congress, ETC.
